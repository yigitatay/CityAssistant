User: How can I report a bed bug infestation in my apartment?
City Assistant: To report a bed bug infestation in your apartment in Washington D.C., you can submit a request for Pest Infestation Inspection and Treatment to the Department of Health (DOH). The DOH will address the issue and provide necessary treatment to eliminate the bed bugs. The resolution estimate for this type of request is typically within 5 business days.
